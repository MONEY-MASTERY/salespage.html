The 4 Phases

Built in a specific order for a reason.

Most entrepreneurs do these in the wrong order — or skip some entirely. This framework sequences every step so each one builds on the last. No wasted effort. No backtracking.

PHASE 1
Foundation
Days 1–7
  • Clarify your idea & problem statement
  • Build your customer persona
  • Validate real market demand
  • Design your business model
  • Competitive analysis & positioning
  • Scope your Minimum Viable Offer
  • Go/No-Go decision with evidence
PHASE 2
Structure
Days 8–14
  • Legal structure & EIN registration
  • Business bank account & accounting
  • Name, domain & social handles
  • Logo & brand identity
  • Website or landing page
  • Email & communication stack
  • Full structure audit
PHASE 3
Build
Days 15–21
  • Build or source your core offer
  • Write your sales message & copy
  • Lead magnet & email sequence
  • Content strategy (2 channels, 3 pillars)
  • Sales pipeline & CRM setup
  • 3-tier pricing with guarantee
  • Launch readiness review
PHASE 4
Launch
Days 22–30
  • Pre-launch buzz campaign
  • Payment & checkout setup
  • Referral & partnership strategy
  • Official launch day execution
  • Customer onboarding system
  • Week 1 analysis & optimization
  • 90-day growth roadmap
